BOTTOM OF THE WISHING WELL
Verse 1
On the streets of Amsterdam she hummed a little tune
Rejoicing in the feeling almost half past noon
She hurries to her love her surprise can't wait a day
Passing on the chit-chat from the people in her way
Chorus
No wicked storm will stop her or raging waters too
No earthquake or tornado firestorm or monsoon
Oh poor lil' Belle at the bottom of the wishing well
Her secret finally told and then her lover turned bold
But don't you fret she's got a mighty hold
Verse 2
Before she reached the well she prepared what she would tell
But the surprise was for her as he confessed another lover
She cried and then she screamed but no one saw the scene
When she turned away he lunged and they both took the plunge
Bridge
He didn't get away as he also fell
Ending up beside her at the bottom of the well
They both saw red and wanted each other dead
In a terrible mess they lay motionless
Chorus
No wicked storm will stop her or raging waters too
No earthquake or tornado firestorm or monsoon
Oh poor lil' Belle at the bottom of the wishing well
Her secret finally told and then her lover turned bold
But he didn't expect she would grip with such a mighty hold
Outro
He didn't trip nothing could loosen her grip
She's stuck like glue and both their wishes came true
Oh what a mess they lay motionless
At the bottom of the wishing well
