BOTTOM OF THE WISHING WELL

(Verse)

On the streets of Amsterdam she hummed a little in her malfulous voice it's almost time to rejoice

She's meeting her man at half past noon

She must hurry to meet her love as it can't wait another day

She passes on the chit chat from the people in her way

No wicked storm will stop her or raging waters too

No earthquake or tornado no firestorm or monsoon

Before she gets to the wishing well she prepares herself on what she will tell

The surprise though will be on her cause he has another lover

(Chorus)

Oh poor lil Belle at the bottom of the wishing well

Her secret she finally told and then her lover turned bold

But don't fret cause she's got a mighty hold

He spoke his truth she yelled then screamed

No one was around to witness the scene

But he doesn't get away as he also fell and ended up at the bottom of the wishing well

Oh poor lil Belle at the bottom of the wishing well

Both of their wishes came true

Oh what a mess they lay motionless at the bottom of the wishing well
