Custom Lyric Commissions

Need original, cinematic lyrics for your next film, commercial, or track? Start here.

Begin Your Project Scoping

To begin the custom commission process, a small, non-refundable scoping fee of **$50.00** is required. This fee covers the initial review of your project brief, a short strategy call, and the preparation of a formal proposal.

$50.00 Scoping Fee

The Process After Payment:

  1. **Project Brief:** You will be immediately redirected to a detailed form to submit your beat link, genre, deadline, and creative vision.
  2. **Assessment:** I will review the brief to confirm I can deliver the high-level quality required.
  3. **Strategy Call:** We will schedule a brief call to align on the narrative and commercial goals.
  4. **Proposal:** If we proceed, the **$50 fee is credited toward the final proposal**, which includes the estimated cost and timeline for the full custom lyric.
