Crafting Sync-Ready Lyrics: A Songwriter's Guide to Commercial Placement

By Kellie Larson, 2025

In today's media landscape, a song's sync potential is a key measure of its commercial viability...

1. Support the Narrative, Don't Steal It

  • Avoid Overly Specific Names and Places: Broad strokes allow flexible placement.
  • Focus on Emotion Over Detail: Let the lyrics evoke the feeling, not tell the whole story.

2. Embrace Lyrical Agnosticism for Genre Flexibility

  • Prioritize Rhythm and Cadence: Natural flow makes adaptation easier.
  • Write for the Hook: The chorus is your sync gold — make it count.

3. Maintain Absolute Professionalism and Clearance Readiness

  • Keep it Clean: Avoid profanity and suggestive content for broader market access.
  • Secure All Splits: Have signed split sheets ready for licensing agents.
  • Be Accessible: Provide clear contact info and respond quickly.
