Beyond the Rhyme: Pro Lyric Formatting

By Kellie Larson, 2025

A well-formatted lyric sheet is a professional calling card. Before sharing with a producer, ensure your layout reflects industry standards to focus attention on your artistry.

What to Include

  • Centered Title: Bolded at the top of the page.
  • Clear Section Labels: Headers for Verse, Chorus, and Bridge.
  • Clean Typography: Use standard fonts like Arial or Helvetica.
