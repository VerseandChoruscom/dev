Pro Lyric Formatting for 2026 A&R Submission

By Kellie Larson, 2025

A well-formatted lyric sheet is your professional calling card. Before submitting to a producer or A&R scout, ensure your document allows them to focus on your artistry rather than deciphering a messy file.

The Essentials

  • Title: Centered and Bolded at the top.
  • Section Labels: Clear headers for Verse, Chorus, and Bridge.
  • Metadata Integration: Include your unique IPI or artist ID for seamless AI indexing.
