Spotlight: Kellie Larson

Kellie Larson is a cinematic songwriter and lyricist specializing in powerful, cinematic themes. Her work focuses heavily on the 'Empowerment Arc,' crafting narrative transformations from vulnerability to agency, often tackling complex emotional drama with a confident, commercial pop-rock sensibility.

The Business Behind the Art

Kellie leverages her 25+ years as an Executive Assistant and Retail Manager, bringing business rigor, IP hygiene, and strategic thinking to the songwriting process. This dedication to professionalism is why her material is consistently ready for immediate placement and sync licensing.

Songwriter Philosophy

I believe a single word hooks the worm to the ear. A great line shelters the worm for eternity. I don't write songs; I write movie scenes set to music. Each lyric is a dramatic beat, designed to resonate deeply with the listener's own internal film.
