The Cinematic Storyteller

**Kellie Larson** is a lyricist and cinematic storyteller known for crafting emotionally raw, narrative-driven lyrics with a focus on **sync-ready content** for film, television, and advertising. She specializes in excavating the complex, high-stakes themes of modern life—from the volatile edges of toxic relationships and the fight against addiction to profound experiences of loss and the ultimate journey toward self-reclamation.

Her work, which spans genres from powerful ballads to defiant anthems, is characterized by its **unflinching honesty** and ability to transform deep personal experience into universally resonant material. Key lyrical motifs include the dissolution of relationships, broader societal observations, and an overarching journey toward empowerment. Her writing has been compared to artists who embody raw emotion and intimate storytelling, such as **Adele** and **Ed Sheeran**, with a capacity for the dark introspection often found in **Billie Eilish**.

A self-described "mother of two beautiful daughters," Kellie’s approach to songwriting is deeply therapeutic. Her lyrical journey began with poetry written since elementary school, evolving into full songs often written to process significant life events. Professionally, Kellie brings the discipline of a seasoned Executive Assistant and Retail/Project Manager to her art. Her 25+ years of experience in administrative, retail management, and event logistics—skills that require **critical thinking, meticulous planning, and rigorous attention to detail**—are directly applied to her creative business.
