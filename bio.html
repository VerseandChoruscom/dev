Kellie Larson

Cinematic Songwriter & Lyricist

Kellie Larson is a cinematic songwriter whose lyrical voice blends emotional authenticity with strategic commercial appeal. Her catalog spans themes of heartbreak, empowerment, social commentary, and fantastical storytelling — often delivered through vivid third-person narratives and defiant arcs of self-reclamation.

A lifelong writer, Kellie began crafting poems in elementary school as a form of emotional expression and storytelling. Her lyrics are therapeutic, grounded in real-life experience, and enriched by deep research — from internet rabbit holes to library biographies and fantasy novels. This layered approach gives her work a unique emotional arc, now recognized as one of the most valuable trends in mainstream music and sync licensing.

Professionally, Kellie operates as a B2B lyricist through platforms like Songbay, where her work is available for commercial licensing and copyright purchase. She specializes in original lyrics paired with AI-generated demos — a strategic choice that positions her as a forward-thinking creator. These demos serve as high-fidelity placeholders, accelerating the pitch process while preserving the human core of her compositions.

Her lyrical strengths shine brightest in third-person narratives like “Wildflower” and “Children of War,” which build immersive worlds and cinematic tension.
