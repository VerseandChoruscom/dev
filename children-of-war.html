CHILDREN OF WAR
Verse 1
The sky is heavy with the weight of lead
Another night of hiding, words left unsaid
Small hands trembling in the cold and dark
Searching for a glimmer, a tiny spark
The echoes of the sirens, a haunting sound
While the world we knew is leveled to the ground
Chorus
We are the children of war, born in the fire
Our dreams are buried beneath the wire
Waiting for a morning that never comes
Marching to the beat of the distant drums
But our voices will rise, a steady flame
In a world that’s forgotten our very name
Verse 2
Empty playgrounds and silent streets
Where the ghost of laughter and sorrow meets
No more lullabies, only the sound
Of the heavy boots upon the ground
We’ve learned to survive, we’ve learned to hide
With a hollow ache deep down inside
Chorus Repeats
Bridge
How many more until the end?
How many broken hearts to mend?
The cost of power, the price of hate
Is written in our bitter fate
Outro
Children of war
We’re just children of war
Waiting for the morning
In the children of war
